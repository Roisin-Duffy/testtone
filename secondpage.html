The 7 New Rules for the 2025 Season

The breakdown on each rule

Goalkeeper Backpass

Goalkeepers can now only recieve the ball back if both players are in the large rectangle of their own goal and was played in by a player from the opposite team or if they crossover into the oppostions half to support the attack. This is to encourage both teams to move the ball forward.

40M Scoring Arc 2pointers

Pitches will now have an additional new arc coming from the 20m line extenting out from goal, When a ball is kicked from outside this arc by a player it is now going to be rewarded with 2 points instead of one. Referees will now identify this is a score by raising his two hands in the air and umpires will wave a orane flag to acknowledge the 2 point score. A 45 is still worth 1 point.This is to encourage the long range shots to be taken.
